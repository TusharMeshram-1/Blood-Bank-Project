I will donate blood

Only if I Am satisfy all of the following conditions:

• I am between age group of 18-60 years.
• My hemoglobin is 12.5 gm% minimum.
• My weight is 45 kgs or more.
• My last blood donation was 3 or more months earlier.
• I Am healthy and have not suffered from malaria, typhoid or other transmissible disease in the recent past.
• My Pulse rate must be between 50 to 100mm without any irregularities.
• My BP Diastolic 50 to 100 mm Hg and Systolic 100 to 180 mm Hg.
• My Body temperature should be normal and oral temperature should not exceed 37.5 degree Celsius.


I will Not donate blood

if I have any of the following conditions:


• Cold / fever in the past 1 week.
• Under treatment with antibiotics or any other medication.
• Cardiac problems, hypertension, epilepsy, diabetes (on insulin therapy), history of cancer, chronic kidney or liver disease, bleeding tendencies, venereal disease etc.
• Major surgery in the last 6 months.
• Vaccination in the last 24 hours.
• Had a miscarriage in the last 6 months or have been pregnant / lactating in the last one year.
• Had fainting attacks during last donation.
• Have regularly received treatment with blood products.
• Shared a needle to inject drugs/ have history of drug addiction.
• Had sexual relations with different partners or with a high risk individual.
• Been tested positive for antibodies to HIV
• Consumed alcohol within the last 24 hours
• Had a dental work for next 24 hours and wait for one month if had a major dental procedure
